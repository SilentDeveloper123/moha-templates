Our Mission
Our Vision
History

To ensure effective public administration, maintain law and order, and deliver quality services to citizens through efficient governance, digital transformation, and disaster management frameworks across all provinces.

  • Efficient local governance through Divisional Secretariats
  • Digital transformation of government services
  • Disaster preparedness and emergency coordination
  • Scholarship programs and community welfare initiatives

A well-governed, disaster-resilient nation with efficient public service delivery, strengthened administrative mechanisms, and citizen-centric governance at all levels of society.

  • Transparent and accountable public administration
  • Technology-enabled citizen services
  • Empowered local communities

Established under Extra Ordinary Gazette Notification No. 1933/13 on 21 September 2016, the Ministry has served as the center of civil administration, facilitation and coordination for related services across Sri Lanka for over seven decades.

  • Established 1931 — over 75 years of service
  • Gazette notification No. 1933/13 (Sept 2016)
  • Headquarters: "Nila Madura", Independence Square
